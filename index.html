Beginner First

If you've never attended a hackathon, or have just picked up programming and want to get a project off the ground, this is the hackathon for you! The Hello World Hackathon is a hackathon created for beginners. Filled with workshops on setting up a project, building it, and testing it. Awesome prizes and food galore! The best part, it's all free!

The University of Edinburgh Programming Society works as a platform connecting students across various disciplines interested in learning to code.
